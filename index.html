2852 High Voltage

FRC Team 2852 is a FIRST Robotics Competition team out of Denis Morris Catholic High School St. Catharines, Ontario. Our team participates in the FIRST Robotics Program at the high school level. We are an innovation driven team composed of an enthusiastic group of students and a set of professional mentors who are dedicated to help these students grow in the field of STEM. Every year, FIRST releases a new game with a variety of different challenges. It is the job of our team to design, build, and program a robot that can complete the tasks that the game outlines. We take our robot and compete against other teams around the world. As a team our goal is to ensure that our students learn the necessary hands-on skills to help them in the STEM field. Not only do we prioritize the hands-on skills, but we strive to create lifelong bonds, strong ideals, confident communication skills, leadership, and productive teamwork skills. Our values go beyond Robots. #MoreThanRobots
