Who We Are

Building Tomorrow's Innovators

FRC Team 2852 is a FIRST Robotics Competition team from Denis Morris Catholic High School in St. Catharines, Ontario.

We are an innovation-driven team composed of enthusiastic students and dedicated professional mentors. Every year, FIRST releases a new game challenge, and it's our job to design, build, and program a robot to compete against teams from around the world.

Our goal extends beyond technical skills. We strive to create lifelong bonds, develop confident communication skills, foster leadership abilities, and build productive teamwork. Our values go beyond robots.
